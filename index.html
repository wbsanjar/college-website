About Us

Technocrats Institute of Technology (TIT) Bhopal is a premier engineering college dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

Courses Offered

  • Computer Science Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

Semester Fees

The semester fees for various courses are as follows:

  • Computer Science Engineering: ₹50,000 per semester
  • Mechanical Engineering: ₹45,000 per semester
  • Electrical Engineering: ₹45,000 per semester
  • Civil Engineering: ₹40,000 per semester

Note: Fees are subject to change. Please contact the administration for the latest information.

Uniform

All students are required to wear the prescribed uniform during college hours.

  • Boys: White shirt, navy blue trousers, black shoes, and college tie.
  • Girls: White salwar kameez or white shirt with navy blue skirt/trousers, black shoes.

Uniforms are available at the college store or authorized vendors.

Attendance

Minimum 75% attendance is required for each subject to be eligible for exams.

Students can check their attendance on the student portal. For any discrepancies, contact the class coordinator.

Regular attendance is mandatory for academic success.
